About Me

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the IT University of Copenhagen since March 2025, with research focused on privacy-preserving machine learning related to intrusion detection systems.

PhD in Computer Science being advised by Dr. Ricardo Jacobi (University of Brasília) and co-advise Dr. Anderson Nascimento(University of Washington at the time of my doctorate) from University of Brasília in 2024.

Master in Computer Engineering, advised by Paulo Barreto from the Polytechnic School of the University of São Paulo in 2010, with a thesis on Analyzing the feasibility of implementing post-quantum algorithms based on multivariate quadratic quasigroups processing platforms is limited.

Bachelor's in Computer Science from the University of Amazonas in 2003.

I am broadly interested in Privacy-Preserving Machine Learning. Currently, my research focuses on privacy-preserving machine learning and cybersecurity.

I like traveling, drawing, reading, and watching movies when not working.
